--- Post 2 ---
I’m proud to share a significant milestone in my academic journey — my research contribution has been officially published in IGI Global Scientific Publishing (2025).

📘 Book: Essential Information Systems Service Management
📄 Chapter: Navigating IT Service Management: Frameworks, Implementation, and Analytical Perspectives

This research focuses on the transformative role of cloud computing in modern enterprises, emphasizing scalability, cost optimization, and enhanced operational efficiency. It also highlights key challenges such as integration complexities, security considerations, and aligning IT services with evolving business strategies.

Through this work, I explored how organizations can leverage cloud-based architectures to drive innovation, improve collaboration, and achieve sustainable growth in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Being part of this publication has been an incredibly enriching experience — strengthening my interest in Data Science, Cloud Computing, and Business Analytics, while deepening my understanding of real-world IT systems and their impact.

Grateful to my co-authors, mentors, and everyone who supported me throughout this journey.

Excited to continue building, researching, and contributing in the domains of AI, Data Science, and scalable systems 🚀
